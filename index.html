🧭About Me

I’m a Robotics M.S. student at CMU’s RI, working with the Soft Machines Lab (Prof. Carmel Majidi) and RExLab (Prof. Zac Manchester). I explore how smart materials and learning-based control can enable agile, energy-efficient aquatic robots. Current interests: variable-stiffness propulsion, fluid–structure simulation, and policy training in the loop.
