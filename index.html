About

I am a Master's student in Robotics at Carnegie Mellon University's Robotics Institute, working with the Soft Machines Lab (Prof. Carmel Majidi) and RExLab (Prof. Zac Manchester).

My research focuses on bio-inspired aquatic robots, variable-stiffness mechanisms, fluid–structure interaction (FSI) simulation, and robot learning for control. I aim to integrate smart materials, optimization, and learning methods to build efficient and adaptive swimming robots.
